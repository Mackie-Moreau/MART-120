Arts and Crafts

Creation is a fickle thing
A mind stuffed with newness cannot comprehend the strange horizons it has entered
With time the world fizzles into view, an understanding which builds upon itself
Yet it will never happen again, not in the same form
The aspect of developing consciousness is idiosyncratic, hard to pin down in a concise manner.

Creation. Such a fickle thing
Nearly esoteric in nature
An abstract impression on the individual
Spiraling strands of thoughts are hard to grasp and harder to express
In a sense,
we are all gods creating our own visions of the world,
A unique perception upon reality that no one else can harbor in thier hearts

If creation is a fickle thing,
Unknowable and untouchable
Divided from convention and collective purpose
Difficult to explain wholly.
Then why shouldn't I make this reality my own?
Find connection in the simplicity of life
Partake in pleasures barred from me by my upbringing
Explore the possibility of change and self expression
Without the constraints of holiness
The strangler fig that wrapped around my throat and barred me from a lived life.
Creation is yours to hold. However fickle it may be.
